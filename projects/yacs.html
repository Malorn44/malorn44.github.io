Yet Another Course Scheduler (YACS)

  • AngularJS
  • Ruby on Rails
  • Typescript
  • HTML
  • SCSS
  • Docker

I worked on YACS, a project under the Rensselaer Center for Open Source (RCOS), from September 2018 - September 2019. I was a project developer from September 2018 - April 2018 and a project lead and RCOS mentor from May 2019 - September 2019. After which, I had to leave the project as I was going to Japan on a study abroad.

Some of the things I worked on during my time at YACS were an admin panel, multi-semester scheduling, and a scheduling sidebar.
